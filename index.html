ABOUT US

Danga Langa was created out of a desire to share experiences, impressions, memories of travel that grought great joy into our lives. Our GLAMPs are an attempt to transfer and provide guest with only positive emotions, which of course "only" complement the holiday mood. The world is amazing, colorful and has a lot to offer. Only WE decide what suits us better. That is why Danga Langa is diverse and different.
